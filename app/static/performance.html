How these are measured

Before velocity = total views at apply ÷ days from publish → apply.
After velocity = views gained since apply ÷ days since apply.
Velocity lift = (after − before) ÷ before × 100. Only computed for videos with ≥7 days post-apply data.
This controls for organic growth — a video already trending will show a "before" rate; the question is whether the rate changed.
